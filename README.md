# Customer Churn Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes **customer churn behavior** to identify why customers leave and to recommend effective retention strategies using demographic, service, and financial data.

## Dataset Description
The dataset includes:
- **Churn** – Customers who left within the last month
- **Services Subscribed** – Phone, multiple lines, internet, online security, online backup, device protection, tech support, and streaming services
- **Account Information** – Tenure, contract type, payment method, paperless billing, monthly charges, and total charges
- **Demographics** – Gender, senior citizen status, partners, and dependents

## Objectives
- Identify the **key drivers of customer churn**
- Provide **actionable steps to retain valuable customers**

## Key Insights
- A significant portion of customers have churned, indicating the need for strong retention strategies
- **Senior citizens** show a higher churn rate
- Customers on **monthly contracts** are more likely to churn than long-term contract customers
- Higher **monthly charges** are associated with increased churn
- Churned customers have **lower total charges** due to shorter tenure
- Customers without **tech support or online security** churn more frequently
- **Fiber optic users** experience higher churn, possibly due to pricing or service issues

## Recommendations
- Encourage **long-term contracts** through discounts and personalized offers
- Improve service quality with enhanced network stability and **24/7 support**
- Offer **free trials** for premium services
- Optimize pricing with **flexible plans** and transparent billing
- Use **predictive analytics** to identify and retain at-risk customers
- Engage high-churn segments with **tailored incentives and plans**
